About Me

My name is Justin Park, and I am currently an MBA student at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF). Prior to graduate school, I attended the University of California, Irvine (UCI) where I received my B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering.

I am currently working as a Program Management Intern at GKN Aerospace located in Garden Grove, CA. My roles include revenue management, forecasting & budget analysis, market analysis, and lifecycle gate management. I take pride in supporting executive decision making with the use of business analytics and business development.

At UCI I was a part of the Kababayan Club where they hosted a multitude of social events, workshops, study sessions, and performance shows. I also enjoyed participating in the intramural sports league at UCI which included both basketball and flag football. During my time in college, I worked a few years in customer service as a server/cook at the restaurant Tender Greens located at the Irvine Spectrum. During my free time I enjoy playing the guitar, watching/playing basketball, and listening to music.
